Welcome to Vicayub Energy Nigeria Limited

Vicayub Energy Nigeria Limited is a distinguished indigenous company operating within the downstream sector of the oil and gas industry in Nigeria. Renowned for its commitment to quality and customer satisfaction, Vicayub Energy specializes in the retail of premium petroleum products, including gasoline (PMS), diesel (AGO), kerosene (DPK), and liquefied petroleum gas (LPG).

Our Mission

"Vicayub Energy Nigeria Limited is committed to delivering premium petroleum products with exceptional service, driving sustainable growth and energy accessibility across Nigeria."

At Vicayub Energy Nigeria Limited, our mission is to provide high-quality petroleum products and unparalleled service to our customers, fostering sustainable development and energy accessibility throughout Nigeria.

Our Services

B2B Retail

At Vicayub Energy Nigeria Limited, we deliver premium petroleum products to businesses nationwide. Our B2B services cater to industries like manufacturing, transportation, logistics, and energy.

End User Retail

We offer top-quality petroleum products to individual consumers across Nigeria. Our retail services ensure reliable and convenient fuel supply for everyday needs, including gasoline, diesel, LPG, and kerosene.

LPG Equipment Retail

We sell a wide range of high-quality LPG equipment for both domestic and industrial use. Our retail offerings include gas cylinders, tanks, cookers, burners, and more, ensuring safe and efficient energy solutions for homes and businesses across Nigeria.
